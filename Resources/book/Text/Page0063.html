Change is inevitable when we encounter God. Like Jacob, my friend Tim began to find healing because he was honest with God—and with others—about who he was; he confessed that his life had become a mess of his own making. When he stopped trying to manage the mess himself and turned it over to God, He found God was giving him a chance at a new identity.

When have you clung to something besides God?

What forced or encouraged you to let go?

Jacob learned that the more he grasped at things, the more he lost. The more he let go, the more he gained.
