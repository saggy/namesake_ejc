To go from a God whose name we’re not even allowed to speak to this God who invites us to use His name every time we pray is a dramatic shift. Yet Jesus reminds us that He is a God not only to be worshipped and revered but also to be approached with our smallest needs as well as our deepest desires.

If we want to know who God is, we need to look at the life and character of Jesus. He is the ultimate self-revelation of God. When Jesus showed up on earth, it was an invitation for us to know God intimately in a way that no one had ever known Him before.
