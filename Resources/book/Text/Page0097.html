How is this different from her reaction in Ruth 1:19-21?

Naomi’s realization that God was working for good on her behalf was a turning point for her. She stopped focusing on the negative past and began to work for positive change for a future for her and Ruth.

In yesterday’s reading we saw how Ruth recognized that if she wanted something to change, she would have to take action. Today we see that all along God has been acting and working behind the scenes. Those two things together are often a recipe for miracles in our lives. We need both God’s powerful action in our lives and our own initiative, joining with Him to work for good. Depending on one or the other ignores the fact that God so clearly wants to work in partnership with us in this world. He is at work for our good, and He wants us to join Him.
