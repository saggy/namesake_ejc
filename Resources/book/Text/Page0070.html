Jesus Christ is the same yesterday and today and forever.
Hebrews 13:8

The integrity of God means that who He is determines what He does. His actions flow out of the goodness, power, and holiness of His name. Likewise, what God does demonstrates who He is. The character of God shows us that we can trust Him. He will never act in ways that are not established in Scripture. God’s actions are consistent with His character.

Read the following Scriptures. What does each reveal about God’s unchanging nature?

Lamentations 3:22-23

Psalm 55:22

John 10:28

Hebrews 13:8

What does this tell us about our ability to trust God?

Perhaps God’s reluctance to articulate His name to scoundrels like Jacob and leaders like Moses had little to do with their ability to control Him by speaking it aloud. Maybe it had more to do with the fact that the nature of God is best discovered not through some word on the tip of our tongues but through following Him, observing His actions, and discovering His nature through His fingerprints in the world.
